Our Story

Crafted with
Intention

Maison Aurel was born from a dream — to create a space where fine dining meets the warmth of a neighbourhood café. Our head chef, trained in Lyon and Tokyo, brings a global perspective to locally sourced ingredients. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries a signature: restraint, balance, and a touch of the unexpected.

We believe food is the most intimate form of hospitality. That's why every detail — from the grain of our oak tables to the weight of our cutlery — is chosen to disappear into the background, leaving only the experience.
